# Global Logistics Product Hub

## Project Description

Global Logistics Product Hub is a decentralized product, inventory, and shipment management platform built on the Stellar Soroban blockchain. The platform enables multinational logistics companies to securely manage products, warehouses, inventory levels, and shipment records across multiple countries through transparent and immutable smart contracts.

By leveraging blockchain technology, every product movement, inventory update, and shipment status change is permanently recorded on-chain, ensuring trust, traceability, and operational efficiency throughout the global supply chain.

## Project Vision

The vision of Global Logistics Product Hub is to establish a transparent, secure, and decentralized logistics ecosystem where manufacturers, warehouses, transportation providers, distributors, and customers can access trusted product information without relying on centralized databases.

The platform aims to reduce fraud, improve inventory visibility, enhance supply chain traceability, and provide a single source of truth for all logistics participants operating across international markets.

---

## Key Features

### Product Management

* Create and manage products securely on-chain.
* Store essential product information including:

  * Product ID
  * SKU
  * Product Name
  * Description
  * Weight
  * Country of Origin
* Update product details with authorized access control.

### Inventory Management

* Track inventory quantities in real time.
* Manage stock across multiple warehouses.
* Record inventory updates and stock movements permanently on-chain.
* Improve inventory visibility across global operations.

### Shipment Tracking

* Create shipment records for logistics operations.
* Track shipment status from origin to destination.
* Maintain an immutable history of shipment updates.
* Enable transparent logistics monitoring.

### Multi-Country Operations

* Support warehouses located in different countries.
* Manage products distributed across international regions.
* Provide global inventory visibility from a single decentralized platform.

### Access Control

* Administrator-controlled product creation and management.
* Authorized logistics staff can update inventory records.
* Public verification of product authenticity and logistics records.

### Transparency & Auditability

* Immutable blockchain records for all logistics activities.
* Complete audit trail for inventory changes and shipment movements.
* Enhanced trust among supply chain participants.

## Usage Instructions

### Step 1: Deploy Contract

Deploy the Soroban smart contract to the Stellar blockchain network.

### Step 2: Initialize Administrator

Assign an administrator account responsible for product and inventory management.

### Step 3: Create Products

Register products with detailed information including SKU, name, weight, and country.

### Step 4: Manage Inventory

Add inventory quantities to warehouses and update stock levels as products move.

### Step 5: Track Shipments

Create shipment records and update shipment status throughout the logistics lifecycle.

### Step 6: Query Data

Retrieve product information, inventory status, and shipment history directly from the blockchain.

### Step 7: Verify Records

Use on-chain data to verify product authenticity and logistics operations transparently.
